Welcome to TaddyEnergy

TaddyEnergy Solutions is a pioneering company that seamlessly integrates the energy sector with the welding industry. By offering specialized energy solutions, including electricity and renewable sources like solar and wind, EnerWeld Solutions ensures that welding operations are both efficient and sustainable. The company also provides advanced welding equipment, technical support, and energy management services, enabling high-quality manufacturing and operational excellence. With a focus on innovation and sustainability, EnerWeld Solutions is committed to meeting the unique energy needs of the welding industry.

Our Services

Home Welding

Home welding allows for DIY metalwork, repairs, and crafting projects in a personal workshop.

Gate Welding

Gate welding involves joining metal components to create or repair gates for security and aesthetics.

Window Welding

Window welding involves fusing metal parts to construct or repair window frames for strength and durability.

Machine Welding

Machine welding automates metal joining processes for precision, efficiency, and high-quality industrial fabrication.

Bike Welding

Bike welding repairs or customizes bicycle frames, ensuring strength and durability for safe riding.

Car Welding

Car welding repairs or customizes vehicle frames and body panels for structural integrity and aesthetics.
